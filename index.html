CONTROLS
Symbols Process 1-2 = Isothermal Compression
Process 2-3 = Constant Pressure Regeneration (internal heat transfer from the working fluid to the regenerator)
Process 3-4 = Isothermal Expansion
Process 4-1 = Constant Pressure Regeneration (internal heat transfer from the regenerator back to the working fluid)
P = Pressure
v = Volume
T = Temperature (K)
Qin = Heat addition from external source
Qout = Heat rejection to external sink
Wnet = Net work output
R (Gas constant of air) = 0.287 kJ/kg.K
Cp (Specific heat of air at constant pressure) = 1.005 kJ/kg.K
Cv (Specific heat of air at constant volume) = 0.718 kJ/kg.K
Efficiency (╖) = Efficiency of Ericsson Cycle Formulae Process 1-2 -> Pv = constant or T = constant
Process 2-3 -> P = constant
Process 3-4 -> Pv = constant or T = constant
Process 4-1 -> P = constant
Qin = R*T3*ln(P2/P1)
Qout = R*T1*ln(P2/P1)
Wnet = -R*T1*ln(P2/P1) + R(T3-T2) + R*T3*ln(P2/P1) + R(T1-T4)
Efficiency = 1-T1/T3
